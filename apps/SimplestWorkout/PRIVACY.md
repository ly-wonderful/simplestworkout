# Privacy Policy

**SimplestWorkout** respects your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

## Data Collection

SimplestWorkout does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All workout plans, exercise logs, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

## Network Usage

The app connects to **wger.de** (a free, open-source exercise database) solely to fetch exercise names when you browse the exercise library. No personal or usage data is sent in these requests.

## Third-Party Services

SimplestWorkout does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Storage

All data remains on your device. Deleting the app will permanently remove all stored data.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at [https://github.com/ly-wonderful/simplestworkout](https://github.com/ly-wonderful/simplestworkout).
