## Description
SimplestWorkout is a clean, no-nonsense workout tracker built for people who just want to log their lifts and get stronger.

Create a weekly workout plan with exercises for each day. When you start a session, the app automatically picks today's routine and starts a timer. Your previous reps and weights show up as placeholders so you always know what to beat.

Key features:

- Weekly plans — organize your entire routine in one place with labeled days (Push, Pull, Legs, etc.)
- Auto day detection — tap Start and the app loads today's exercises automatically
- Live workout timer — tracks your session duration from start to finish
- Previous session recall — see your last reps and weight as gray placeholders in every field
- Exercise database — browse and search from thousands of exercises powered by wger.de
- Full history — review past sessions with duration, exercises, sets, and weights
- Simple profile — switch between lbs and kg with one tap
- Sample PPL program — import a ready-made Push/Pull/Legs program to get started instantly

No account required. No ads. No subscriptions. Your data stays on your device.
