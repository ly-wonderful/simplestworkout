# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 7, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how SimplestWorkout ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our iOS application.

---

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect your **email address** and a **password** (stored as a secure hash). This information is processed by Firebase Authentication (Google LLC) to verify your identity and maintain your session.

### Workout & Health Data
Your workout plans, session logs, exercise history, and body weight entries are stored **locally on your device** using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is not transmitted to our servers.

### API Key
If you choose to use the exercise browser feature, you may enter a RapidAPI key. This key is stored locally on your device and is used solely to make requests to the ExerciseDB API on your behalf.

---

## How We Use Your Information

- **Email address** — to authenticate you, maintain your session, and allow password reset.
- **Workout and weight data** — to display your history, statistics, and progress within the app. This data never leaves your device.
- **RapidAPI key** — to fetch exercise data from ExerciseDB when you use the exercise browser.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

---

## Third-Party Services

### Firebase Authentication (Google LLC)
We use Firebase Authentication to manage user accounts. When you sign up or sign in, your email address is sent to Firebase servers. Google's privacy policy applies to this data: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### ExerciseDB (RapidAPI)
If you use the exercise browser, your search queries and RapidAPI key are sent to RapidAPI's servers to retrieve exercise data. RapidAPI's privacy policy applies: https://rapidapi.com/privacy

---

## Data Storage & Security

- Workout plans, sessions, and weight logs are stored **only on your device** and are not backed up to our servers.
- Account credentials are managed by Firebase Authentication using industry-standard security practices.
- Your RapidAPI key is stored locally on your device.

---

## Data Retention & Deletion

- You can delete your account at any time by contacting us (see below). This will remove your authentication data from Firebase.
- Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all locally stored workout data, weight logs, and settings from your device.

---

## Children's Privacy

SimplestWorkout is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
